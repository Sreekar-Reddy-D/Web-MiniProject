INSTRUCTIONS

Instructions

  • To solve the sliding puzzle, arrange the numbered blocks in numerical order from left to right, top to bottom.
  • Click on a block adjacent to the empty space to move it into the empty space.
  • Continue moving blocks until all blocks are in the correct numerical order.
  • The puzzle is considered solved when all blocks are in the correct order.

How to Play:

  • Click on the "EASY", "MEDIUM", or "HARD" difficulty button to choose the level of difficulty.
  • Start by clicking on the blocks adjacent to the empty space to move them and solve the puzzle.
  • Use logical thinking and strategic moves to rearrange the blocks and complete the puzzle.

Tips:

  • Start by moving blocks around the empty space to create pathways for other blocks.
  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting stuck.
  • Practice patience and persistence to solve the puzzle efficiently.

Have Fun!

  • Enjoy the challenge of solving the sliding puzzle and improving your problem-solving skills.
  • Don't hesitate to reset the puzzle or change the difficulty level if needed.
